Premium Feature: Automated Match Recording

Stand out from the competition with our professional match recording system. We install a high-quality camera at your field that automatically captures every match booked through Playmaker.

🎥

Smart Camera Technology

Professional recording, zero effort

  • 🎬 Fully Automated Recording
    The camera automatically starts recording when a booked match begins and stops when it ends. No manual operation needed—completely hands-free.
  • 📹 High-Quality HD Video
    Professional-grade camera captures clear, high-definition footage from the perfect angle. Players can relive their best moments in crystal-clear quality.
  • ☁️ Cloud Storage & Instant Access
    All recordings are automatically uploaded to secure cloud storage. Players can watch their matches within minutes after the game ends—right from their phones.
  • ✅ Zero Cost to You
    We install, maintain, and manage the entire camera system at no charge. Installation, maintenance, repairs, and upgrades are all covered by Playmaker.
  • 🔧 Professional Installation & Maintenance
    Our technical team handles everything—from mounting the camera safely to ensuring it works perfectly. We perform regular maintenance to keep it running smoothly.
  • 🛡️ Your Field, Your Property
    While the camera belongs to Playmaker, the footage of your field is available to you. Access recordings through your Admin App anytime.
  • 📱 Full Access Through Admin App
    View all recordings from your field. Download footage for promotional videos, social media content, or highlight reels. Great free marketing material!
  • 🎁 Marketing Advantage
    Fields with recording cameras attract more bookings. Players love watching their matches and are willing to pay premium rates for this exclusive feature.

🌟 Premium Experience = Premium Bookings

Players specifically search for fields with recording cameras. This feature sets your venue apart, attracts more customers, and allows you to charge higher rates. It's a complete win-win.

How It Works

1. We install the camera at your field (one-time setup)
2. Camera syncs with booking schedule automatically
3. Recording starts when matches begin, stops when they end
4. Footage uploads to cloud automatically
5. Players receive their match video within minutes
6. You can access all recordings through your Admin App
